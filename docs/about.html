Why Algorithmic Trading?

Trading has a reputation for being gambling because humans are emotional. We remove the human element to treat trading as a computer science problem.

Feature Manual Trading Algorithmic Trading
Execution Speed Human reaction time (~300ms) Instant execution (<5ms)
Emotion Fear & Greed affect decisions 100% Logic & Math
Consistency Varies by mood/sleep Identical execution 24/7
Backtesting Guesswork or manual checking Verified on historical data
Sleep Misses opportunities overnight Trades while you sleep
